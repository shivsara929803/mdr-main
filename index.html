our services

Cashew Nut Shell Liquid

One source of naturally occurring phenols is Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL). The shell of a cashew nut is used to make it.

CNSL Resin

CNSL Resin is a clear viscous resin with a golden colour film that is suited for surface coating applications due to its film characteristics and strong resilience.

Cardonal

Cardanol is a renewable & inexpensive organic natural resource that is easily obtained via the vacuum distillation of roasted cashew nut shell liquid (CNSL).
